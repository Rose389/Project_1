Dress Code

For all Classes
Unless otherwise specified by the instructor all students should wear black nylon leotards, pink full-footed tights, and appropriate footwear for dance style.
Swing/Ballroom
Students should wear comfortable clothing with hard soled, steady shoes. Ladies may wear a small heel.
Pilates/Barre Sculpt
dance or yoga attire. Clothing must be fitted to body and allow for movement, no shorts.
Boys
Black cap-sleeve shirts and solid black pants. All dance style shoes should be black.
Siblings must be supervised in the lobby area.
Adults
Typically wear the standard uniform. if deviating for any reason, wear solid colors in dance or yoga attire that allows instructor to see ankle, knee and shoulder movement.
