Preteen & Teen Classes

Classical Ballet (Ages 5+)
The foundation of all dance, classical ballet teaches grace, poise, posture, strength, flexibility, builds self discipline, and coordination. This form should be studied first and in conjunction with other forms of dance.
Pointe (By invitation only)
Factors include age, ability, and maturity. Ballet required as well.
Jazz (Ages 7+)
A highly stylized form of dance using the body for self expression. This is a modern dance form that is more rhythmic, fun, and commercial. Jazz styles are seen on television and in videos and movies and consist of lyrical, hip hop, and funk combinations. Usually added as an addition to ballet & tap.
Tap (Ages 5+)
Rhythmically oriented, taps enhances memory and promotes quick thinking, fast foot work and flexible feet.
Irish style (Ages 7+)
Soft show and hard shoe taught. Usually added after adding jazz.
Charm Course (Ages 6-17)
Modeling, make-up, interview, introductions, and other etiquette skills.
Gym/Tumbling (Ages 2+)
Designed to strengthen and limber the body, to develop technique, and to master tricks including rolls, cartwheels, backbends, hand springs, limbers, and aerials.
Drill Team (Ages 13+)
A prep class to condition anyone who wants to try out for DT or improve high school and college dance skills
Pilates (12+)
Focuses on the core postural muscles and awareness of breath which helps keep the body balanced and is essential to providing support for the spine. This becomes a must for the advancing dancer. It also useful for dancers who have sustained an injury or want to avoid common dance injuries, body therapy.
Barre Sculpt (12+)
For the individual needing additional strengthening in dance, utilizing the barre, rings, balls, bands, etc..
Private Lessons (All Ages)
Inquire about private lessons for competition prep, special help, choreography, spring concert prep, or pageant coaching.
Company Class (By invitation only)
Our Company Class performs at senior centers, in competitions, and in our Spring Concert.
Master Class (By invitation only)
Guest instructors instruct classes in technique and teach routines.
